Faculty from Cheju Halla University recently visited Adobe Research in San Jose, where they had the opportunity to examine several innovative projects. Among these, the Adobe Stardust project was particularly impressive. This is an AI-driven editing engine capable of object recognition, and it is poised to be a game-changer in shifting from a layer-based to an object-based approach when adopting AI tools.

A particularly striking aspect of the visit was Adobe's investment in clean, accurate data. This approach sets a new benchmark and underscores the importance of reliable datasets for generative models. Adobe's prominence in this area is a compelling illustration of the synergy between data integrity and AI effectiveness.

Also noteworthy was the vibrant and advantageous working environment at Adobe Research. An environment where innovation, creativity, and collaboration come together is truly inspiring, and one can easily imagine contributing professionally and growing in such a place.

This visit was a precious opportunity to directly experience Adobe Research's innovative AI technology and outstanding data management practices. These technologies and approaches are expected to continue driving the advancement of the industry going forward.
