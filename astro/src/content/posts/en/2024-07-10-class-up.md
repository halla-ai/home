Students from the Department of Artificial Intelligence at Cheju Halla University achieved outstanding results at the 2024 Semester 1 CLASS-UP Competition under the 'Local Government-University Cooperation-Based Regional Innovation Project.'

At the competition held at Maison Glad Jeju on July 2, our department's students earned top scores, demonstrating their deep understanding of AI technology and creative application skills.

## Department of Artificial Intelligence Award Winners

- **Individual Grand Prize**: Yang Pilseong
- **Individual Excellence Award**: Kim Jaeun

These awards are the result of our department's students receiving external recognition for their AI technology capabilities and innovative problem-solving abilities. In particular, claiming both the Grand Prize and Excellence Award in the individual category is an achievement that demonstrates the educational quality of the Department of Artificial Intelligence and the outstanding skills of its students.

## Significance of the Competition

The CLASS-UP Competition is a venue where students from diverse majors participate to present and compete with knowledge from their areas of specialization. Through this competition, our department's students gained valuable experience in:

1. Presenting real-world AI technology application cases
2. Exploring possibilities for convergence with other fields
3. Improving presentation and communication skills

## Future Development

The award-winning students have earned priority participation in various extracurricular programs offered by the Jeju Regional Innovation Platform Intelligent Services Business Division during the summer vacation. Through this, they are expected to:

- Acquire the latest trends in AI-related technologies
- Participate in industry-linked projects
- Gain experience in the global AI ecosystem

The Department of Artificial Intelligence plans to continue providing sustained support and innovative education programs so that students can grow into leading talents in the AI field, building on these achievements. Our department will fulfill its role as a cradle for cultivating core talent to lead AI technology development and innovation in the Jeju region.
