## Theme

Develop an AI that predicts whether a credit card user will default on their payment

## Description

- Develop an algorithm that predicts credit card payment defaults using credit card user data
- The online qualifying round is a process to verify participants' data analysis and AI utilization abilities, selecting 20 teams to advance to the offline finals
- The offline finals will consist of 30 teams in total: 20 teams selected from the Dacon online qualifying round plus 10 Jeju-based teams invited by the organizers
- The offline finals will be held over 1 night and 2 days in Jeju, with the same theme as the online qualifying round

## Organizer / Host / Operator

- Organizer: Jeju RIS Intelligent Services Project Group
- Host: Cheju Halla University, Jeju National University, Jeju Tourism University
- Operator: Dacon

## Eligibility

Any high school student residing in Korea (teams of 2 to 5 members)
