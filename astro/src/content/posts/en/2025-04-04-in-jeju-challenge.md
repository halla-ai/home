At the '2024 COSS In-JEJU Challenge' competition held from December 20 to 23 at the Halla Convention Center of Cheju Halla University, the 'Nangnanghan Expression Lab' team (Advisors: Prof. Young Joon Lee, Prof. Yongjun Chang, Prof. Ha Ran Kim), composed of students from the Department of AI (Chair: Prof. Cho Hye-jin), won the Grand Prize. In addition, the 'CHL' and 'KHK' teams each received honorable mentions.
