Cheju Halla University is pleased to announce the opening of a new Artificial Intelligence Center equipped with the latest NVIDIA DGX H100 AI supercomputers. This state-of-the-art facility will further strengthen our capabilities in AI research and education.

**Key Features:**

- **Latest Equipment**: The new AI Center is equipped with two NVIDIA DGX H100 AI supercomputers, complementing the existing DGX A100, DGX 1, and multiple RTX A6000 GPU servers.
- **Powerful Computing Capabilities**: This cutting-edge equipment provides researchers and students with unprecedented computing power, enabling them to tackle complex AI challenges and drive innovation across diverse fields.
- **Support for Diverse Research**: Supporting interdisciplinary research projects in natural language processing, computer vision, robotics, autonomous systems, and more, promoting both academic and applied AI research.

The opening of this AI Center was made possible through the support of the Jeju RIS platform. The university plans to continuously expand its computing capabilities to train and deploy state-of-the-art AI models.

**Expected Outcomes:**

- **Innovative Research Results**: The AI Center will produce innovative research outcomes and contribute to the advancement of AI technology.
- **Advancement of Education and Research**: It will open a new chapter in further developing AI research and education at Cheju Halla University.

Through the opening of the AI Center, Cheju Halla University will firmly establish its position as a leader in the AI field. We will support our researchers and students in utilizing this state-of-the-art facility to conduct world-class research.
