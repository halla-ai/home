## Educational Goals

As the only ICT-specialized department in the province, we train professional technical personnel equipped with both applied skills and flexible field adaptability required by the rapidly growing intelligent systems industry — including Artificial Intelligence (AI), Big Data, Security (Blockchain), and Robotics.

## Course Plan

- Enhance learning outcomes through major lectures that combine theory and hands-on practice.
- Conduct special lectures and mock exams to ensure thorough preparation for various certifications.
- Build real-world experience through internships and field practice.
- Improve individual students' major competencies through activation of startup and major clubs.

## Differentiation Strategy

- **Educational Differentiation**: Learner-led learning systems such as team project-based learning, creative maker education, and flipped learning, along with integrated learning processes that enhance creativity and problem-solving.
- **Field-Centered Differentiation**: Curriculum focused on developing practical project execution skills reflecting the needs and trends of local industries.
- **Interdisciplinary Linkage**: Curriculum developed through interdisciplinary and inter-major convergence with the university's core departments such as Nursing & Health Sciences, Tourism & Hotel Management, and Horse Industry.
