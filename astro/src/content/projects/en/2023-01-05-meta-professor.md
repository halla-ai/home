A research project utilizing meta-learning techniques to identify professors in academic papers and develop an AI-powered education platform based on these findings.

## Research Objectives

- Automated generation of personalized educational content using Generative AI and Large Language Models (LLMs)
- Building a transparent and trustworthy education system through Explainable AI
- Supporting diverse learning modalities via multimodal interaction technology

## Core Technologies

- **Generative AI**: Content generation tailored to individual student learning needs
- **Cognitive AI**: Learning pattern analysis and personalized feedback
- **LLM-based Dialogue System**: Natural language Q&A support for learning
