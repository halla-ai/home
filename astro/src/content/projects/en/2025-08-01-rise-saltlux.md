This is an Industry-Academia joint technology development project carried out together with Saltlux, Yonsei University, and KAIST as part of the RISE (Regional Innovation System & Education) program.

## Research Objectives

By building a next-generation intelligent educational platform that fuses Agentic RAG technology with Socratic dialogue methods, we aim to improve hallucination issues and enhance learners' critical thinking. We will innovate the Jeju region's specialized industry-academia cooperative education ecosystem and provide a 24-hour AI tutoring service.

## Core Technologies

- Design and development of **Multi-Agent-based Agentic RAG** architecture
- Construction of a **Socratic dialogue management system**
- **Knowledge Tracing**-based personalized learning support
- **Constitutional AI**-based educational ethics safeguards
- Development of **simulation learning environments** linked to Jeju's strategic industries
- Construction of an agent **reasoning evaluation framework** and benchmarks
