We are proud to introduce the MetaHuman AI Instructor. This innovative educational platform, developed by combining Unreal Engine 5 and ChatGPT-4, will take the future of education to the next level.

## Project Overview

The **MetaHuman AI Instructor** achieves a vivid and realistic appearance and movement through Unreal Engine 5's cutting-edge graphics technology. The AI instructor, which converses and acts as naturally as a real person, sets a new standard in education.

### Key Features

1. **Realistic Graphics and Animation**

   - **Unreal Engine 5** is used to realistically render the MetaHuman AI instructor's appearance and movements.
   - Sophisticated animation blueprints and sequencers are used to smoothly express a variety of actions.

2. **Intelligent Conversation System**

   - By integrating **ChatGPT-4**, the MetaHuman AI instructor understands user questions and provides contextually appropriate answers.
   - It flexibly handles various educational scenarios, making lectures and Q&A sessions richer.

3. **Context-Aware Action Execution**

   - The MetaHuman AI instructor performs specific actions in response to user commands. For example, when asked to "jump," the MetaHuman actually jumps.

4. **Natural Voice Interaction**

   - Using the Eleven Labs Voice Model, the MetaHuman AI instructor reads text in a natural voice just like a real person.
   - All conversations take place directly through a microphone, providing an experience of talking with a real instructor.

5. **Lecture Content Learning and Explanation**
   - The MetaHuman AI instructor pre-learns PPT slides and lecture materials and can explain specific sections in detail. For example, it accurately explains in response to a question like "Tell me about question 3."

## Expected Impact

- **Innovation in Education**: By interacting with students and providing personalized learning experiences, it enables more immersive and engaging learning.
- **Cost Reduction and Improved Accessibility**: By providing high-quality virtual instructors at low cost, various educational institutions and students can utilize this innovative tool.
- **Lowering Technical Barriers**: Designed to be easy to use without complex technical knowledge, enabling anyone to create creative content.
